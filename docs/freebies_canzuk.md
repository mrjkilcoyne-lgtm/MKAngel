- **GenZone US LLC/EIN** -- US entity only; CANZUK Ltd is UK.
- **topeducation.uk / UK Edu Info** -- personal student finance, not business.
- **airmedclinic** -- personal medical cannabis.
- **Tallee (Monzo overdraft refund)** -- personal claims management, takes a cut.

## Avoid -- scam / predatory patterns

- **HT Blue Network Page** -- "GBP 100-500/day side income for Ltd owners" is a textbook fraud hook, often a front for VAT/dividend abuse schemes.
- **Portman Finance Growth Guarantee** and **Rise Funding UK GBP 250k** -- aggressive loan brokers leaning on "government backed" / "no credit impact" language. The underlying Growth Guarantee Scheme *is* real (British Business Bank), but go **direct to an accredited lender**, not via brokers who tack on fees and hard-search you later.
